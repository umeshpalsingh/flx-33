We are a family owned business that serves our community through the love of the cross.

Our divine connection supports our passion to serve each other.

We are available to YOU to create heartfelt results throughout our studio!

Our Vision

We provide an inviting space with intention and unconditional love.

This special community is bonded through the love of the cross, inside and outside of our studio.

Most gyms don’t want you to show up…we don’t want you to ever miss a workout!

Mission Statement

We have created a sacred space in our community, just for you! Our PURPOSE is to support you in your personal fitness journey.

This special community is born out of a love of the cross in and outside of the studio.

Our Goal is to help you find PEACE and positive energy that will give you the POWER to be the best version of YOURSELF

OUR CLASSES

Our programing is focused on YOUR mind, body, & spirit. With our state of the art technology and intentional coaching, FLX 33 supports your personal journey with care. All of our classes are created with purpose and passion. We care deeply about bringing the very BEST to you!

OUR COMMUNITY

As we ride with intention, we also focus on spreading love into our world! We are a judgemental free and faith based community. You will feel guided and loved inside our studio, which we call, our Sanctuary or our Sacred Space

GIVING BACK

Giving back to our community is one of the FLX33 core beliefs. Part of living the healthy lifestyle is giving to our community with fundraising and scholarships. You will see us donating to local projects that are close to our hearts

Our Instructors

Kim Steckman, Co-Owner

Kim has been in the fitness industry  for 30 years. She is a personal trainer, group fitness instructor and holds a variety of certifications.   She has competed at the National level in Figure and enjoys running marathons and weight Training. Kim is passionate about using fitness to  inspire others to develop the discipline they need to see all their goals accomplished!!

Dustyn Steckman

Dustyn has had over 10 years experience training.   He holds several certifications including TRX, Group Fitness  and, Spinning. He is an avid runner and especially enjoys competing in marathons and obstacle runs and weight training.   Dustyn is known for his passionate motivation and intense style!!
